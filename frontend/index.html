Trust, safety and responsibility

KisanMitraAI does not promise guaranteed yield, price, buyer payment or profit. We use ranges, evidence, confidence scores and admin review to support better decisions.

🧪

Estimate vs verified data

Soil and satellite intelligence are labelled as estimates unless verified by lab tests or expert review.

📷

Crop-cycle evidence

Photos, GPS, time, crop stage and quality records help build buyer confidence and reduce fraud risk.

🏛️

Scheme guidance

Government scheme information should include official source, eligibility, documents and last verified date.
